# MesaFlow — CEO Notes

## Business Discovery Summary

MesaFlow is a restaurant SaaS focused on reducing chaos at the door by replacing paper waitlists with a simple QR-based queue system.

The initial problem is real and personally observed:

- Restaurants lose potential customers because walk-in queues are poorly managed.
- Customers wait without clarity.
- Staff become stressed.
- Existing solutions are often archaic: paper, caderno and verbal communication.

## Initial Market

First geographic focus:

- Barreiro.
- Setúbal.
- Seixal.

First restaurant types:

- Marisqueiras populares.
- Restaurantes turísticos.
- Tascas/grelhados movimentados.

## Initial User Flow

1. Customer arrives without reservation.
2. Receptionist points to QR code.
3. Customer scans QR.
4. Customer enters name, phone and number of people.
5. Customer receives confirmation that they are in the queue.
6. Customer can see their position in the queue.
7. Staff sees the waitlist.
8. When table is ready, staff clicks “Call Customer”.
9. Customer receives WhatsApp message.
10. Staff seats the customer.

## Important Product Decisions

- Customer should self-enter via QR.
- Entry should be automatic, without staff approval.
- QR code should initially be available only inside/on-site at the restaurant.
- Manual staff entry should exist as a fallback.
- Wait-time estimation should not be included at launch.
- WhatsApp notification should be part of the MVP.
- SMS can be considered as fallback if needed.
- The product must not require customers to install an app.

## Core Messaging

Main promise:

> Menos caos à porta. Mais mesas ocupadas.

Supporting message:

> Transforme a fila do restaurante numa experiência simples e profissional.

Sales language should emphasize:

- Operational efficiency.
- Less stress.
- Fewer lost customers.
- Better customer experience.
- Simplicity.

## Objections Expected

Likely objections:

- “Sempre fizemos assim no papel.”
- “A minha equipa não vai usar.”
- “Não quero mais uma mensalidade.”
- “Os clientes não vão usar QR.”
- “Não preciso disto todos os dias.”

## Counter-Positioning

MesaFlow is not a complicated new system.

It is a simple replacement for the paper waitlist.

The best response to objections is not argument. It is a low-risk pilot.

## Pilot Strategy

Offer:

- 30 days free.
- No commitment.
- Founder-led setup.
- QR code provided.
- Feedback requested.
- Testimonial/case study requested if successful.

## Conversion Strategy

After 30 days, ask:

- Did the team use it?
- Was it easier than paper?
- Did it reduce confusion?
- Did customers understand it?
- Do you want to keep it for 29€/month?

## Product Quality Bar

MesaFlow cannot fail at:

- Speed.
- Simplicity.
- Reliability.
- Not losing names.
- Not sending wrong notifications.
- Not confusing reception staff.
- Not creating more work than paper.

## Strategic Warning

Avoid premature expansion.

Restaurants may ask for reservations, table maps, menus or POS integrations. These should be noted but not immediately built.

The business is not validated until restaurants pay and keep using the waitlist.

## CEO Phase Conclusion

The first company objective is not to build a complete restaurant platform.

The first company objective is to prove that restaurants will pay for a simple QR waitlist that staff actually use during real service.
