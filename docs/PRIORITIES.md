# MesaFlow — Priorities

## Current Phase

Business Discovery is complete.

The next phase should focus on validating the simplest product and sales motion.

## Priority 1 — Validate Demand

Goal:

Prove that restaurant owners/managers recognize the problem and accept a 30-day pilot.

Actions:

- List target restaurants in Barreiro, Setúbal and Seixal.
- Prioritize restaurants with visible queues or dinner peaks.
- Prepare short founder sales pitch.
- Offer 30-day free pilot with no commitment.
- Track objections.

## Priority 2 — Validate Staff Adoption

Goal:

Prove that receptionists/front-of-house staff actually use MesaFlow during peak service.

Success criteria:

- Staff use MesaFlow without heavy training.
- Staff do not return to paper during peak periods.
- Staff say it is simple or useful.
- Staff trust the list.

## Priority 3 — Validate Customer Flow

Goal:

Prove that waiting customers can scan QR, join the queue and understand their position without staff intervention.

Success criteria:

- Customers scan QR successfully.
- Customers enter name, phone and party size.
- Customers understand confirmation.
- Customers understand queue position.
- Customers respond when called.

## Priority 4 — Validate Willingness To Pay

Goal:

Convert pilot restaurants into paying customers.

Initial success target:

- 5 paying restaurants within 90 days.

Conversion trigger:

- Restaurant used MesaFlow during real service.
- Staff accepted it.
- Owner/manager sees value.
- Restaurant agrees to pay 29€/month.

## Priority 5 — Create Proof Assets

Goal:

Build trust for future sales.

Assets needed:

- Testimonials.
- Case studies.
- Before/after story.
- Receptionist quotes.
- Owner/manager quotes.
- Simple numbers from usage.

## Priority 6 — Decide Next Product Expansion

Only after the core waitlist loop is validated, evaluate:

- Reservations.
- Table management.
- Wait-time prediction.
- Advanced WhatsApp automation.
- Reports for owners.
- Multi-location features.

## What To Deprioritize Now

Do not prioritize:

- Full reservation engine.
- Complex table map.
- POS integrations.
- Loyalty systems.
- Customer app.
- Marketplace.
- Advanced analytics.
- Enterprise sales.
- Broad national marketing.

## Priority Rule

Anything that does not help reach 5 paying restaurants in 90 days should be treated as secondary.
