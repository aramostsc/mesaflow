# Empty States

## No active service
Title: **No service is open**  
Body: Open a service to start receiving and managing the waiting list.  
Action: **Open service** (authorised users only).

## Active service, empty queue
Title: **No customers waiting**  
Body: Add a walk-in customer or share the restaurant’s QR code.  
Primary: **Add customer**. Secondary: **View QR code** where permitted.

## No called customers
Use a quiet inline state within the Called section: **No customers called.**

## No history
Title: **No completed services yet**. Avoid suggesting configuration changes.

## Search without results
Title: **No matching entries**  
Body: Check the spelling or clear filters.  
Action: **Clear filters**.

## No permissions
Do not present this as empty data. Use an access-denied state and route to Queue or account.

## Public closed intake
Title: **The waiting list is closed**  
Body: This restaurant is not accepting new entries right now. Contact the restaurant directly for assistance.
