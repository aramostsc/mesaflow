# MesaFlow — North Star

## North Star Metric

**Customers successfully called through MesaFlow per restaurant per week.**

## Why This Metric Matters

This metric captures real usage and real value.

A customer successfully called means:

- The customer joined the queue.
- The restaurant used MesaFlow during service.
- The waitlist was active.
- Staff trusted the system enough to call the customer.
- The product participated in a real seating flow.

It is stronger than signups, QR scans or restaurant accounts because it measures the completed operational loop.

## Supporting Metrics

### Activation Metrics

- Restaurants onboarded.
- QR codes deployed.
- First customer joined.
- First customer called.
- First full service day using MesaFlow.

### Usage Metrics

- Customers joining waitlist per restaurant per week.
- Customers called per restaurant per week.
- Active waitlist days per restaurant per week.
- Peak-hour usage.
- Manual staff entries.
- Customer cancellations.

### Retention Metrics

- Restaurants active weekly.
- Restaurants active after pilot.
- Restaurants still active after 60 and 90 days paid.
- Receptionist feedback score.
- Owner/manager willingness to continue paying.

### Business Metrics

- Pilots started.
- Pilot-to-paid conversion.
- Paying restaurants.
- Monthly recurring revenue.
- Churn.
- Average revenue per restaurant.
- Cost of WhatsApp/SMS per restaurant.

### Qualitative Metrics

- Receptionist says it is easier than paper.
- Owner says the restaurant looks more organized.
- Customers understand the QR flow.
- Staff do not abandon MesaFlow during busy periods.

## 90-Day Success Definition

MesaFlow is on track if, within 90 days, it reaches:

- 5 paying restaurants.
- Regular usage during peak service.
- At least 1 strong case study.
- Positive receptionist feedback.
- Evidence that restaurants will pay after the pilot.

## 12-Month Success Definition

MesaFlow succeeds in year one if it reaches:

- 50 paying restaurants.
- Clear retention.
- Repeatable sales process.
- Strong evidence that the core waitlist product is valuable.

## Metrics To Avoid Overvaluing

Do not overvalue:

- Number of accounts created.
- Number of QR scans without queue entries.
- Feature requests.
- Website traffic.
- Social media interest.
- Demo compliments.

The product is only working if restaurants use it during real service and continue paying.
