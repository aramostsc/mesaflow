# MesaFlow — Mission

## Mission Statement

MesaFlow helps busy restaurants manage walk-in queues simply, professionally and without paper, so teams can reduce front-door chaos and customers know where they stand.

## The Problem We Solve

Many restaurants still manage waiting customers with paper, memory, verbal estimates or improvised processes.

This creates:

- Customers waiting without clarity.
- Customers leaving before being seated.
- Stress for reception and front-of-house staff.
- Confusion during peak dinner service.
- Poor customer experience before the meal even begins.
- A less professional first impression.

## Who We Serve

MesaFlow initially serves:

- Busy urban restaurants.
- Tourist restaurants.
- Popular seafood restaurants, tascas and grilled-food restaurants.
- Restaurants with high dinner demand.
- Restaurants in Barreiro, Setúbal and Seixal as the first geographic focus.

The initial buyer is usually the owner or manager.

The daily user is primarily the receptionist or front-of-house staff member managing arrivals.

The secondary user is the end customer waiting for a table.

## Mission Principles

Every decision must help the restaurant:

- Move faster.
- Stay organized.
- Reduce stress.
- Avoid losing waiting customers.
- Communicate more clearly.
- Look more professional without needing complex software.

## First Product Mission

The first version of MesaFlow must do one thing extremely well:

> Let customers join a restaurant waitlist by QR code and let staff call them when the table is ready.

## Mission Boundary

MesaFlow should not initially attempt to solve reservations, full table planning, POS integration, CRM, loyalty, marketing automation or marketplace discovery.

Those can be considered later only if they support the core mission and do not compromise simplicity.
